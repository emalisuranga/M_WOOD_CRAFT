Welcome to Furniture Gallery, your one stop shop for furniture.

At Furniture Gallery we all come to work with only one goal- To bring your furniture of your dream into the reality.

Established in 1985, we specialize in designing and manufacturing furniture and accent pieces for homes, restaurants, hotels and more. With more than 30 years of experience in hands, we know the best when it comes to furniture manufacturing in Sri Lanka. We believe the secret to our long-term success is the quality and unique design of our products which we continue to maintain in the years to come.

When we design furniture, we make sure you are the only one to own that design in the world. When we manufacture furniture, we make sure to deliver you a quality and neat product worth your every rupee.

Working with the few of the best furniture designers and woodworkers in Sri Lanka we are here to make your unique furniture dream come true.

Contact us today and share your future dreams with us!
